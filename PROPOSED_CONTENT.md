### **Focus Area: Pharmacy Procurement & Supply Chain**
*Turning Challenges into Competitive Advantages*

Pharmacy operations often represent a significant portion of a hospital's revenue stream yet face critical challenges—from supply chain gaps to price variations.

**The Challenge:**
*   **Complex Markets:** Navigating branded, generic, and unbranded options while avoiding spurious products.
*   **Inefficient Procurement:** High volume does not always equality better pricing without skilled negotiation and market intelligence.
*   **Operational Bottlenecks:** Issues with inventory days, stock-outs, and logistics can erode margins.

**The MedGo Solution:**
We act as your dedicated procurement arm, ensuring:
*   **Direct Manufacturer Access:** collaborative purchasing to secure the best rates.
*   **Optimized Inventory:** Strategies for minimal inventory days and zero stock-outs.
*   **Formulary Management:** Ensuring an approved hospital formulary with zero expiry risk.
*   **Margin Improvement:** Directly enhancing EBITDA and hospital profits through smarter buying.
